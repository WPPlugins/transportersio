Easily add Transporters.io quote forms to your site. Ideal for minibus, coach and bus hire / charter, private hire and airport transfers.
 
== Description ==

Add a quote / booking form to your website quickly and easily. If you don’t already have the free Transporters.io transport management system get it [here](https://transporters.io/wordpress/)

Customers no longer want to email a quote request and wait days for a reply. They expect instant pricing, booking and confirmation. Transporters.io supports any transport operator and this plugin has custom script and style control to easily add tracking scripts and other modifications to match the design of your website.

Badly designed quote forms costs you business - we did lots of testing to see exactly where, when and why customer stop filling in the form and go elsewhere. As a result of extensive ongoing research we have created the highest converting quote form on the market. Install Transporters.io today and watch your enquiries increase.

### What is Transporters.io?

Transporters.io is an online booking, business and communication management tool built specifically for the transport industry. It is optimised for group transport and private hire but also adapts for a lot of different uses.

* Coach hire
* Bus charter
* Minibus hire
* Airport transfers
* Contract hire
* Taxi services
* Car hire
* Air charter
* Yacht charter
* Logistics
* and much more.....

If you just want to ability to generate instant online quotes and collect bookings and payment 24 hours per day then go ahead and install the plug in, configure your Transporters.io system for your business and away you go.

### What can it do?

Transporters.io helps you run your transport business smoothly and efficiently, keeping all of your data securely in one place and automating your most time consuming tasks.

* Take bookings through your website by adding quote forms using this plugin.
* Price jobs manually or automatically using pricing rules that adapt to your business.
* Locate address through google maps and google places
* Take payments online through paypal or several other payment providers.
* Email customers on demand, on a schedule or triggered by events, all without leaving the system.
* Assign drivers to jobs and notify all parties concerned.
* Market leading simple drag and drop scheduling screen for daily driver allocations.
* Generate repeating jobs automatically based on your desired schedule.
* Full reporting and accounting solutions, including exports to cloud accounting solutions such as Xero.
* GPS tracking via Android drivers mobile app.
* and much more.....

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who should install our plugin =

Our plugin is only useful for subscribers of [Transporters.io](https://transporters.io/wordpress/)
Our software is free so if you run a transport company but don't yet use Transporters why not give it a try.

= What if I already use another system? =

Unfortunately this plugin only works with Transporters.io so you wont be able to connect it to your existing transportation booking management system.

However Transporters.io is very easy to learn, very powerful and the most affordable product on the market, starting at FREE!
If you have access to your existing data we can assist with getting your past bookings moved across making the switch very simple.

So if you currently use distinctive systems, sengario, ecoachmanager, roeville, dte, the booking system or any other dispatch / despatch solution we suggest you signup for a free account, give Transporters.io a trial run and speak to our helpful staff about switch over!
